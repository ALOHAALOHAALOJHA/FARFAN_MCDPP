## Instructions for Evaluators

### Overview

You are evaluating a Colombian Territorial Development Plan (Plan de Desarrollo Territorial - PDT) to assess its coverage of 10 policy areas across 6 evaluation dimensions.

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Criteria |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **3.0** | EXCELENTE | Comprehensive, explicit, actionable content with clear targets, indicators, and budget allocation |
| **2.5** | - | Strong coverage with minor gaps |
| **2.0** | BUENO | Substantial coverage with some gaps in specificity or actionability |
| **1.5** | - | Moderate coverage, general statements present |
| **1.0** | ACEPTABLE | Basic mention, lacks detail or concrete actions |
| **0.5** | - | Minimal reference, vague or tangential |
| **0.0** | INSUFICIENTE | No evidence or extremely limited (< 1 mention) |

### Dimension Definitions

| DIM | Name | What to Look For |
|-----|------|------------------|
| **DIM01** | Diagnóstico y Recursos (Insumos) | Baseline data, current situation analysis, resource inventory |
| **DIM02** | Diseño de Intervención (Actividades) | Proposed actions, programs, strategies |
| **DIM03** | Productos y Outputs | Deliverables, outputs, tangible results |
| **DIM04** | Resultados y Outcomes | Expected changes, behavioral/institutional outcomes |
| **DIM05** | Impactos de Largo Plazo | Long-term societal impacts, systemic change |
| **DIM06** | Teoría de Cambio (Causalidad) | Logical framework, causal reasoning, if-then logic |
