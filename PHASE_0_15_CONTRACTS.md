## The 15 Dura Lex Contracts

Based on the contractual framework in dura_lex/, here are the 15 contracts:

1. **alignment_stability.py** - Output alignment must remain stable across runs
2. **audit_trail.py** - All operations must be auditable
3. **budget_monotonicity.py** - Budget allocations must be monotonic
4. **concurrency_determinism.py** - Concurrent operations must be deterministic
5. **context_immutability.py** - Context objects must be immutable
6. **failure_fallback.py** - Failures must have defined fallback behavior
7. **governance.py** - Governance rules must be enforced
8. **idempotency_dedup.py** - Operations must be idempotent
9. **monotone_compliance.py** - Compliance scores must be monotonic
10. **permutation_invariance.py** - Results invariant to input permutation
11. **refusal.py** - System must refuse invalid requests
12. **retriever_contract.py** - Retriever must satisfy contract
13. **risk_certificate.py** - Risk must be certified
14. **routing_contract.py** - Routing decisions must be traceable
15. **traceability.py** - All decisions must be traceable
