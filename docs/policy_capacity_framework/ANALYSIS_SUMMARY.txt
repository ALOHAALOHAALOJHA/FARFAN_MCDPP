   W_agg = η × exp(-λ × Δ_level)
   Parameters: η=0.85, λ=0.15

   M_equip = 1 + δ × ln(1 + n_skills) × (ρ - 1)
   Parameters: δ=0.3

4. Systemic Capacity Score:
   C_systemic = Σ(C_base_i × W_agg_i × M_equip) / N
